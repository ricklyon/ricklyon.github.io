Serves 2 people.

## Ingredients

* 1 cup oats
* 1 tsp. baking powder
* 1/4 tsp. salt
* 3/4 tsp. cinnamon
* 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1 tsp. vanilla
* 1 tsp. sugar
* 1 egg

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Pour into bowl and let sit for 5 minutes to thicken up.
2. Cook pancakes as usual on greased skillet.
