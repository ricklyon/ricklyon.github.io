Makes about one cup of homemade ranch, best used for salads and not as a dip

## Ingredients

* &frac34; cup sour cream
* &frac12; cup whole milk
* 1 Tbsp lemon juice
* 1 Tbsp dried parsley
* &frac12; tsp salt
* 1 tsp minced onion
* &frac12; black pepper
* &frac18; dried dill
* &frac18; garlic powder


## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients and serve!
